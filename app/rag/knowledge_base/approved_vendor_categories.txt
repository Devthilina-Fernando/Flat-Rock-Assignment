1. PURPOSE

This register defines the approved vendor category framework. Categories determine the level of due diligence required and the applicable approval pathway. Categories must be assigned by the Procurement team; vendors may not self-assign a category.

2. CATEGORY TIER DEFINITIONS

  TIER 1 — PRE-APPROVED (STREAMLINED ONBOARDING)
  Vendors in Tier 1 categories are subject to a streamlined 5-business-day review. Standard compliance documents apply. Enhanced due diligence is not required unless specific risk factors are identified. Procurement Manager approval is sufficient for contracts up to £100,000.

  TIER 2 — STANDARD APPROVAL
  Vendors in Tier 2 categories follow the standard onboarding process (10–15 business days). Full compliance documentation required. Approval authority per contract value thresholds in PROC-POL-001.

  TIER 3 — ENHANCED DUE DILIGENCE
  Vendors in Tier 3 categories require enhanced due diligence including detailed financial assessment, enhanced sanctions and adverse media screening, site visit or reference checks (minimum two independent references), and legal review of contract terms. Minimum 20 business day review regardless of contract value. CFO or Director sign-off required for all Tier 3 engagements.

  NOT APPROVED — ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION
  The Company does not engage vendors operating in these categories under any circumstances. Not Approved status is absolute and cannot be overridden without a Board resolution.

3. TIER 1 — PRE-APPROVED CATEGORIES (STREAMLINED)

  CAT-T1-001: Cloud Infrastructure & Hosting Services
    Sub-categories: IaaS, PaaS, cloud storage, CDN, data centre colocation, managed cloud hosting, disaster recovery as a service (DRaaS)

  CAT-T1-002: Software as a Service (SaaS) — Business Applications
    Sub-categories: ERP systems, CRM platforms, project management tools, HR information systems (HRIS), finance and accounting software, collaboration tools

  CAT-T1-003: Software Development & Engineering Services
    Sub-categories: Custom software development, mobile application development, API development, QA and testing services, DevOps consultancy, technical architecture

  CAT-T1-004: Cybersecurity Services
    Sub-categories: Managed Security Operations Centre (SOC), vulnerability management, penetration testing, endpoint detection and response (EDR), security awareness training, SIEM managed services

  CAT-T1-005: IT Hardware & Peripherals
    Sub-categories: Laptops, desktops, monitors, networking equipment, servers, storage devices, printers, peripherals, IT accessories

  CAT-T1-006: Telecommunications & Connectivity
    Sub-categories: Mobile contracts, fixed-line telephony, broadband and leased line connectivity, VoIP services, unified communications

  CAT-T1-007: Office Supplies & Consumables
    Sub-categories: Stationery, paper products, printer consumables, cleaning supplies, kitchen consumables, small office equipment

  CAT-T1-008: Utilities
    Sub-categories: Electricity, gas, water supply for Company premises

4. TIER 2 — STANDARD APPROVAL CATEGORIES

  CAT-T2-001: Professional Services — Consulting
    Sub-categories: Management consulting, business strategy, organisational design, change management, interim management, operational improvement

  CAT-T2-002: Professional Services — Legal
    Sub-categories: Commercial legal advice, employment law, IP and licensing, dispute resolution, regulatory compliance legal advice
    Note: All legal suppliers must be SRA-regulated or equivalent recognised body.

  CAT-T2-003: Professional Services — Finance & Accountancy
    Sub-categories: Audit services, tax advisory, financial modelling, bookkeeping, payroll processing, VAT consultancy

  CAT-T2-004: Marketing & Creative Services
    Sub-categories: Digital marketing, SEO and PPC, brand strategy, graphic design, content production, copywriting, PR and communications, events management

  CAT-T2-005: Facilities Management
    Sub-categories: Office cleaning, building maintenance, security guarding, reception services, waste management, pest control, HVAC servicing, fire safety and compliance

  CAT-T2-006: Recruitment & Staffing
    Sub-categories: Permanent recruitment, temporary staffing, contract staffing, executive search, RPO services

  CAT-T2-007: Training & Development
    Sub-categories: Technical training, leadership development, compliance training delivery, e-learning platform provision, apprenticeship support

  CAT-T2-008: Logistics & Courier Services
    Sub-categories: Same-day courier, parcel delivery, freight, international shipping, document courier

  CAT-T2-009: Data & Analytics Services (Non-Sensitive)
    Sub-categories: Market data subscriptions, business intelligence tools, data visualisation platforms, industry benchmarking services
    Note: Where services involve processing personal data, reclassification to Tier 3 may apply — refer to DPO.

  CAT-T2-010: Research & Development — External Partners
    Sub-categories: University research partnerships, technical research agencies, innovation and patent research

5. TIER 3 — ENHANCED DUE DILIGENCE CATEGORIES

  CAT-T3-001: Data Processing — Personal Data (High Volume or Sensitive)
    Sub-categories: HR data processing, health and medical data processing, financial data processing on behalf of the Company, customer personal data platforms, data brokers, identity verification services
    Rationale: High risk of data protection regulatory exposure. DPO sign-off mandatory.

  CAT-T3-002: Payment Processing & Financial Infrastructure
    Sub-categories: Payment gateways, acquiring banks, e-money institutions, card processing vendors, billing and collections platforms
    Rationale: PCI DSS, FCA regulation, financial crime risk. Legal and CFO sign-off required.

  CAT-T3-003: Outsourced Business Process (BPO) — Customer-Facing
    Sub-categories: Customer service outsourcing, contact centre services, complaints handling, customer data management
    Rationale: Reputational risk; significant personal data exposure; regulatory scrutiny potential.

  CAT-T3-004: Legal Compliance & Regulatory Advisory
    Sub-categories: FCA regulatory advisory, GDPR advisory, employment tribunal support, anti-bribery compliance consultancy
    Rationale: Privileged information access; regulatory sensitivity.

  CAT-T3-005: Mergers, Acquisitions & Corporate Finance Advisory
    Sub-categories: M&A advisers, investment banks, corporate brokers, due diligence advisers
    Rationale: Highly sensitive commercially; conflict of interest risk; board-level engagement required.

  CAT-T3-006: International Suppliers (Non-UK/Non-EEA)
    Sub-categories: Any goods or services sourced from suppliers headquartered outside the UK and EEA
    Rationale: Enhanced sanctions screening; data residency risk; modern slavery supply chain risk. Legal review of applicable governing law required.

6. NOT APPROVED CATEGORIES

The following categories are outside the scope of permitted vendor engagement. Submissions in these categories will be rejected without further review.

  CAT-NA-001: Cryptocurrency and Digital Asset Services
    Includes: Cryptocurrency exchanges, crypto wallet providers, blockchain-based payment processors, DeFi platforms, NFT platforms, crypto mining services, cryptocurrency investment advisory, cryptocurrency portfolio analytics, on-chain data intelligence, distributed ledger verification services, blockchain-based data verification

  CAT-NA-002: Online Gambling & Betting
    Includes: Online casino operators, sports betting platforms, lottery services, gaming software for gambling purposes

  CAT-NA-003: Tobacco and Tobacco Alternatives
    Includes: Tobacco product suppliers, e-cigarette and vaping product suppliers for resale

  CAT-NA-004: Weapons, Defence Ordnance & Lethal Autonomous Systems
    Includes: Firearms suppliers, ammunition, weapons components, cluster munitions, lethal autonomous weapons systems

  CAT-NA-005: Payday Lending & High-Cost Short-Term Credit
    Includes: Payday loan providers, high-cost credit products, unregulated lending platforms, rent-to-own schemes

  CAT-NA-006: Adult Content Services
    Includes: Adult entertainment platforms, adult content creation services, adult product retail

  CAT-NA-007: Multi-Level Marketing (MLM) Structures
    Includes: Any supplier whose primary commercial model involves recruitment-based commission structures

  CAT-NA-008: Unregulated Investment Products
    Includes: Unregulated collective investment schemes, unauthorised financial promotions, exotic/speculative investment product vendors not FCA-authorised

7. CATEGORY REVIEW

Category assignments are reviewed annually. Business units may request reclassification by submitting a Category Review Request to the Head of Procurement. Reclassification of a Not Approved category requires Board approval.
