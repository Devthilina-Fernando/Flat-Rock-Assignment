ABOUT GLOBALDATA ANALYTICS LTD

GlobalData Analytics Ltd is a UK-registered technology and data services
company specialising in the delivery of advanced analytics, on-chain data
intelligence, and distributed ledger verification services. Founded in
2019, we serve clients across financial services, fintech, supply chain,
and enterprise software sectors.

Our flagship platform, ChainVerify Pro, combines traditional business
intelligence reporting with blockchain-based data verification and
cryptographic audit trail functionality. We help organisations gain
verifiable, tamper-proof insights from their operational and transactional
data.

We are registered in England and Wales and fully compliant with all
applicable UK legislation.
